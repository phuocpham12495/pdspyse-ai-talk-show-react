## [User] input a topic, tone level, and conversation length
**Benefit:** generate a structured AI talk show episode with distinct Comedian and Expert personas

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The user can specify the talk show topic.
- The user can select a tone level (light, balanced, deep debate).
- The user can choose the conversation length (short, medium, long).
- The generated output includes an opening intro, back-and-forth discussion, highlight moments, and a closing summary.
- The Comedian persona exhibits witty, sarcastic, and entertaining dialogue.
- The Expert persona exhibits logical, data-driven, and thoughtful dialogue.
